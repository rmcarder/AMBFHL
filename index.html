4/11 Tournament Information

Champions: TJ and Travis!

Tournament format and rules:

  • 2v2 tournament where losers start a 1v1 for redemption.
  • During round robin play, all teams play each other once with 3 minute periods.
  • Bracket games are 4 minute periods
  • All games are superstar difficulty.
  • Mercy may be called in a 3+ goal game in the 3rd period by the trailing team.
  • Top two teams in round robin get a first round bye in the bracket.
  • Half beer/half wine/half shot per goal. Large waters per goal cost $2 each, which will go to the winner of the bracket.
  • Enter all scores into the Google form linked at the top. In addition to the information for this tournament, the scores will start building our All Time Standings. Use Round Robin game type for pool play, Bracket game type for the bracket, and Championship game type for the Championship.
