4/4 Tournament Information

8pm start time. Tournament format and rules:

  • First 4 rounds are randomized teams and matchups. Schedule is below. These games will have 4 minute periods.
  • Individual players will receive wins/losses and the total number of goals for and against even though the games are 2v2 and 2v1.
  • The top 8 players after the 4 rounds of pool play will enter a single elimination 1v1 bracket. Tiebreakers for top 8 and for seeding is by goal differential. Periods for the bracket are 5 minutes.
  • Half beer/half wine/half shot per goal. Large waters per goal cost $2 each, which will go to the winner of the bracket.
  • Enter all scores into the Google form linked at the top. In addition to the information for this tournament, the scores will start building our All Time Standings. Use Round Robin game type for pool play, Bracket game type for the bracket, and Championship game type for the Championship.
